FAQ's

1. How Do Premium SMS Services Factor Into SMS Marketing Pricing Structures?

Textdrip’s tier-based pricing includes various features across different plans, ensuring that businesses can access premium SMS services tailored to their needs without hidden costs. From Spark Starter to Elite, our plans offer scalability and affordability for all businesses.

2. What Is A2P 10DLC Regulation In SMS Marketing Costs?

A2P 10DLC (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) is a regulatory framework imposed by carriers to ensure the legitimacy and quality of SMS traffic. Compliance with A2P 10DLC regulations may involve additional costs for businesses sending marketing messages via SMS.

3. Can Businesses Expect SMS Marketing Costs To Vary Based On Industry Or Niche?

Yes, SMS marketing costs may vary based on factors such as industry regulations and the specific features required. With Textdrip’s tiered pricing structure, businesses can choose a plan that best fits their industry and marketing needs.

4. What Are The Regulations And Registration Requirements For Signing Up On Textdrip?

Since you’ll be assigned a 10-digit number, you’ll have to comply with the 10 DLC regulations to avoid message delivery issues for an enhanced messaging ecosystem. To comply with these regulations, you’ll be required to complete your EIN registration to register your business as an entity and then register your SMS campaigns with The Campaign Registry authorities. This is to ensure the privacy of users and let them know who’s messaging them and about what.

5. Is Textdrip Available Outside Of The United States?

Currently, our SaaS SMS marketing software serves only to businesses registered in the United States. So, if you and your audiences reside in America, you can use the platform and reach them through SMS.

6. Are There Any Hidden Fees Associated With SMS Marketing Cost?

All the carrier charges and other fees are covered in your monthly & annual subscription plan and the credits you purchase. There are no hidden fees or charges so you don’t have to worry about anything. For more help, contact our sales team.

7. How Does SMS Marketing Pricing Compare Across Different Service Providers?

Compared to other service providers, Textdrip offers competitive tier-based pricing plans tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses. Our monthly and annual subscription options provide flexibility and cost-effectiveness.

8. Can You Provide A Breakdown Of The Cost Of SMS Marketing Per Message?

Our pricing is based on tiered subscription plans rather than a per-message breakdown. The cost per message varies depending on the plan you select, ranging from the Spark Starter plan at $19.99 per month to the Elite plan at $74.99 per month. Annual plans offer discounted rates compared to monthly subscriptions.

9. What Happens When My Text Messages Are Over 160 Characters?

When your text message limit exceeds 160 characters, it’ll be counted as multiple segments. Multiple segment messages have a limit of 153 characters per segment and will attract a charge of 1 credit per segment.

10. How Can I Estimate My Credit Purchase Needs?

The best way to estimate your credit purchase requirements is to look at your current SMS subscriber list and how often you need to message them. For instance, let’s say you have 1200 contacts and want to message them all once a week (up to 160 characters), then the total credits required in a month will amount to around 4800 to 5000. You can do the math accordingly and purchase more credits as and when required with the flexibility of the pay-as-you-go pricing model.

11. What Is A Credit And How Much One Credit Is Worth?

Consider credits as a form of currency for using our SMS marketing platform. It is used for measuring the number of messages you send to each contact. When you send 1 text message of 160 characters per segment, it’ll consume one credit. However, it may vary depending on the number of segments in one message or multimedia attachment included. Textdrip charges $0.012 for one credit, meaning $12 for 1000 credits.

12. How Much Does SMS Marketing Cost

The cost of SMS marketing with Textdrip depends on the tier you choose. Our monthly plans start at $19.99 for Spark Starter, $34.99 for Growth Gear, and $74.99 for Elite. Alternatively, our annual plans offer discounted rates: Spark Starter at $191.88, Growth Gear at $335.88, and Elite at $719.88.
